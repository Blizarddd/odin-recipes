The Perfect Egyptian Rice with Vermicelli

Description:

Egyptian rice (roz bel shareya) is one of the staples of Egyptian cuisine. This popular rice cooked with vermicelli noodles is a hearty dish that pairs wonderfully with many recipes, but is typically eaten alongside meat (such as lamb) or vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • ¼ cup 1/2-inch long vermicelli¼ cup 1/2-inch long vermicelli
  • 1 cup Egyptian rice (short-grain rice)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 ¾ cups water
